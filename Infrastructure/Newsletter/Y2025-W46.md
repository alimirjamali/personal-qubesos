We had a recent patch to allow accepting only specific HID input devices (keyboards & mice) based on the vendor and the model. With this new patch, filtering based on device serial number is also possible. However, many devices might not provide a serial number at all (e.g. my old IBM mouse and Logitech LX3). I did not manage to contact the PR author to query about the recommended models which might provide a serial number. This could be disabled by setting `input-proxy-exclude-serial` future of USBVM. You may find serial of USB devices by running `sudo lsusb -v | less` on your USBVM and look for `iSerial` field.
